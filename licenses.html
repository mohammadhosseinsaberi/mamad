Licenses

As a trusted provider in the construction materials industry, we are fully licensed and certified to operate in both domestic and international markets. Our licenses and certifications reflect our commitment to quality, compliance, and customer satisfaction."

Assets

ISO 9001:2015 Certification
Rozhan Trading holds the internationally recognized ISO 9001:2015 certification, which demonstrates our commitment to quality management, high manufacturing standards, and service processes. This certification ensures the premium quality of our products, including travertine, marble, marble tiles, and quartz.

International Export License
Rozhan Trading holds export licenses to over 30 countries, including the United Arab Emirates, Turkey, Iraq, Kuwait, Germany, France, the United Kingdom, and South Africa. Our products are exported to various markets across Europe, the Middle East, Central Asia, and Africa, and are highly regarded for their exceptional quality and customer satisfaction.

Environmental and Social Responsibility Certifications
As part of our commitment to environmental sustainability and international standards, Rozhan Trading also holds ISO 14001 and OHSAS 18001 certifications, which demonstrate our adherence to environmental and health safety standards in the production and export of our building stones.

CE Marking
Our stone products, such as travertine and marble, are CE certified for markets in the European Union and Central Asia. This certification indicates compliance with EU safety, health, and environmental requirements. The CE mark assures that our products meet international standards for safety and quality.
